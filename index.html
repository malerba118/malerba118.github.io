Giftstr.com

Giftstr.com

Giftstr is a gift recommendation service.


It is intended for personal use and as a way of finding gifts for others.

Each person starts out with the same list of product suggestions. You, the user, assume the identity of each person in your persons list and like or dislike product suggestions based on your knowledge of this person. In time, we learn each person's preferences and give suggestions based on their likes.

Be lenient when liking products. Just because a person already owns an item or because an item is costly, doesn't mean you should skip it. This is important because we use a person's likes to determine their suggestions.

Our system does this using a method known as collaborative filtering. In simple terms, we find persons that are most similar to the target person and then suggest to the target person items that these similar persons have liked in the past.
